Mauhallewale is a Production and Design Company specialising in creating tailored content for its clients. Our aim is to create a digital asset which digs its heels deep into the client’s vision, and at the same time, brings out the vision in the most creative way possible. Digital content can imply anything, ranging from a thirty second commercial to a six minute event aftermovie. But the challenge is to make sure that the viewer’s eye does not go off the screen. The company has been started in 2015 by a group of graduate enthusiasts from DCE, sharing a common belief that in this competitive arena, the content and the advertisements are going to be the game changers.
